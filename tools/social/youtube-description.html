Description Length Guidelines

                    

                        
    
                            
  • Minimum: 200 characters (basic SEO value)
    • 
                            
  • Recommended: 200-300 words (optimal for SEO)
    • 
                            
  • Maximum: 5,000 characters (YouTube limit)
    • 
                            
  • Above the fold: First 100-150 characters are most visible
